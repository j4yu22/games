Combat Sequence
    Import Character sheet
    Make adequate combat encounter
    Roll initiative
    Each entity gets a call of turn()
        each turn allows for one action, bonus action, and movement.
            list of actions:
            - attack
            - cast spell
            - shove
            - grapple
            - dash
            - hide
            - disengage
            - use object
        bonus action and movement are tracked as resources
    combat ends when either the player or creatures have 0 hp
